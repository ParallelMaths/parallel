## 2.
::: problem id=2_1 marks=1
<!--- PMC 2006 (3) --->
Among the children in a certain family, each child has at least one brother and at least one sister.  

What is the _smallest_ possible number of children in the family?  

* [ ] 2
* [ ] 3
* [x] 4
* [ ] 5
* [ ] 6
{.col-5}

---

There must be at least two boys and two girls in the family.  

If, for example, there was exactly one boy, then that child would not have a brother.  

The same argument applies to girls.
:::


## 3.
::: problem id=3_1 marks=1
<!--- JMC 2004 (5) --->
The word 'thirty' contains 6 letters and 6 = 30 รท 5.  

Similarly, the word 'forty' contains five letters and 5 = 40 รท 8.  

Which of the following is not a multiple of the numbers of letters it contains?  

* [ ] Six
* [ ] Twelve
* [x] Eighteen
* [ ] Seventy
* [ ] Ninety
{.col-5}

--- 

Eighteen has 8 letters and 18 is not a multiple of 8.  

Of the other options, 6 is a multiple of 3, 12 is a multiple of 6, 70 is a multiple of 7 and 90 is a multiple of 6.
:::
