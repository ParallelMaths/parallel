# Parallel Academy Preparation Paper

This paper contains 25 questions on a range of topics that we will be covering in January. It is optional but by working through these questions you will get a good sense of the kind of maths you will be studying on the Tutorial Programme (the problems will get tougher in January so think of this as a warm-up paper).

It will probably take you about an hour to complete and you do not need to do it in timed conditions or in a single sitting - take however much time you need.

After you have submitted your answers, you will be able to see which questions you got right/wrong, as well as worked solutions. **Do not submit until you have properly thought about every question.**

Some questions are harder than others. If you are totally stuck then it’s fine to leave some questions blank (but don’t give up too quickly - perseverance is key in maths!).

Every question is worth 1 mark but don’t worry too much about your score. Instead, **review the solutions carefully to see where you went wrong**.

We hope you enjoy attempting this paper and we look forward to tackling more problems with you in January!

**Before you click Submit at the end of the assignment, check that all of your answers have been entered correctly.** 
  
**After you click Submit, make sure you scroll down to see the correct answers and read the full written solutions.**
